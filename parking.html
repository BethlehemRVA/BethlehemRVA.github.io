PARKING

PARKING OPTIONS

Limited street parking is available on streets surrounding the church.

Handicap parking is available adjacent to the church on Ryland Street.

Free parking is available on Sundays in the VCU West Broad Street Parking Deck.

After parking in the deck and getting your ticket, you can walk to the church using either West Broad Street or West Grace Street (see map). Parking passes for the parking deck are provided each year in June and are available for pickup in the narthex or church office. If you do not have a parking pass, you can ask the pastor to validate your parking ticket.
