Events

Monthly Community Dinners

On the last Sunday of each month, Bethlehem serves a free dinner to those in the community. This allows the church to interface with the community in the atmosphere of a family meal.

Annual Summer Yard Sale

Throughout the year, Bethlehem gathers household goods from our members and others who wish to donate. Each September, we host a yard sale to sell these goods to benefit the community dinner program.

Sandwich Sundays

Every Sunday after the Divine Service, church members assemble peanut butter and jelly sandwiches for the Ram Pantry at VCU. The sandwiches are then distributed to VCU students in need by the Ram Pantry.

Easter Sunrise Service

We celebrate the Resurrection of Our Lord by gathering at Dogwood Dell Amphitheater by candlelight before dawn, and praising Him as the sun rises upon Easter Sunday. This event is open to the public.
