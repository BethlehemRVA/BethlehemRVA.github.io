Museum of the Bible

Saturday, March 11, 2023

Bethlehem Lutheran Church is organizing a trip to the Museum of the Bible for Richmond area Lutherans. The trip will take place on Saturday, March 11, 2023. We will meet in the parking lot of Trinity Lutheran Church at 6:30am. We will return at 7:00pm. The cover charge for this event is $25.00, payable through our PayPal. Please include "Museum of the Bible Trip" as a note when making your payment. Registration ends February 15, 2023.
